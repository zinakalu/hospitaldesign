About Nova Health

Nova Health Medical Center is a nationally recognized leader in shaping health outcomes through innovative research, education and compassionate patient care. Based in Abuja, Nova Health Medical Center is an integrated, nonprofit health system with more than 900 teammates serving patients at 10 hospitals and more than 200 care locations.

Our Care Continuum

At Nova Health, we deliver the full spectrum of care, with locations and services that include:

  • Hospitals
  • Academic medical center
  • Freestanding emergency departments
  • Urgent care centers
  • Physician practices
  • Behavioral health centers
  • Home health care
  • Pharmacies
  • Imaging centers
  • Laboratories
